PVC Chain Link Fence

PVC chain link fences are a versatile and long-lasting fencing solution that excel in durability and longevity. They are manufactured using galvanised steel wires that are coated with a protective layer of PVC. This coating helps prevent corrosion, rust, and weathering, which makes the fencing more durable and long-lasting. PVC chain link fences are available in a variety of heights, wire gauges, and colours, to suit a host of needs. Be it securing your properties, defining boundaries, or creating a stylish enclosure, Nirmal PVC chain link fences are an excellent choice. They are strong, durable, and require minimal maintenance.

APPLICATIONS

Residential Security

PVC coated chain link fences offer a cost-effective and durable solution for residential security. They provide a clear view of the property while deterring intruders. The PVC coating enhances the fence's appearance and protects it from rust and corrosion, ensuring long-lasting performance.

Commercial Property Perimeter Security

PVC coated chain link fences are widely used to secure commercial properties such as warehouses, factories, and industrial parks. They offer a robust and visible barrier, deterring unauthorised access and protecting valuable assets. The PVC coating adds a professional aesthetic and enhances the durability of the fence.

Sports Field Enclosures

PVC coated chain link fences are ideal for enclosing sports fields like soccer fields, baseball fields, and tennis courts. They provide a clear view of the game while keeping spectators and players safe. The PVC coating offers excellent weather resistance and minimises maintenance requirements.

School and Playground Safety

PVC coated chain link fences are commonly used to create safe and secure perimeters around schools and playgrounds. They prevent unauthorised access, protect children from potential hazards, and provide a clear view for supervision. The PVC coating adds a colourful and appealing aesthetic to the school environment.

Animal Enclosure

PVC coated chain link fences are suitable for enclosing animals such as dogs, cats, and livestock. They provide a secure and visible barrier, preventing animals from escaping and protecting them from predators. The PVC coating is resistant to chewing and scratching, ensuring the fence's longevity.
