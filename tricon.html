Tricon® (High Strength Deformed Steel Bars)

Tricon® is a high-strength deformed steel bar engineered for exceptional structural integrity. Its unique three-sided rib design ensures optimal bond strength with concrete, while its superior tensile strength, precise dimensions, ductility, weldability, and seismic resistance make it a reliable and cost-effective solution. Tricon® is widely used in various construction projects, from residential buildings to infrastructure, offering enhanced performance, durability, and safety.

FEATURES

Material

Tricon® Bars are made from high quality steel.

Sizes

Tricon® Bars are available in 3.50-10.45 mm.

Standards

Tricon® Bars conform to IS 1786:2008 (Grade Fe550)

Tensile Strength

Tricon® Bars are rated to tensile ratings of 585 MPa

Packaging

Tricon® is available in bundles. Bundle quantity can be customised to requirement.

APPLICATIONS

Reinforcing Concrete Columns

Tricon®'s high tensile strength and superior bond strength make it ideal for reinforcing concrete columns, ensuring their structural integrity and load-bearing capacity.

Constructing Reinforced Concrete Beams

Tricon®'s excellent ductility and precise dimensional tolerances allow for efficient bending and shaping, making it suitable for reinforcing concrete beams in various structural applications.

Building Foundations

Tricon®'s durability and resistance to corrosion make it a reliable choice for reinforcing concrete foundations, providing a solid base for structures in diverse conditions.

Constructing Reinforced Concrete Slabs

Tricon®'s versatility and high tensile strength allow it to be used in reinforcing concrete slabs for floors, roofs, and other structural elements.

Reinforcing Bridge Piers and Abutments

Tricon®'s superior bond strength and resistance to weathering make it suitable for reinforcing bridge piers andabutments, ensuring their long-term durability and structural integrity.
