Niznal® Vineyard Wire

NIZNAL® Vineyard Wire is a durable, corrosion-resistant wire ideal for vineyards. Its unique zinc-aluminium alloy coating offers superior protection against rust and weathering. The wire's self-healing properties further enhance its longevity. Available in various sizes and strengths, NIZNAL® Vineyard Wire is perfect for supporting grapevines and maximizing yield.

FEATURES

Composition

NIZNAL® vineyard wires are made from premium steel wires coated with a 90%zinc and 10% aluminium alloy.

Diameter Range and Coating

NIZNAL® vineyard wires are available in wires ranging from 1.6-4mm with coatings ranging from 70-650 GSM

Tensile Strength

NIZNAL® vineyard wires display exceptional tensile strength ranging from 700-800 MPa

Surface Finish

NIZNAL® vineyard wires have a smooth and uniform surface finish that is free of defects or imperfections.

Standards

NIZNAL® vineyard wires are made according to a range of international standards which include ASTM, BS and DIN.

Packaging

NIZNAL® vineyard wires are packaged in 50 kg coils.

APPLICATIONS

Vineyard Support systems

NIZNAL® Vineyard Wire is ideal for building robust and long-lasting vineyard support systems. It can be used for both vertical and horizontal wires, providing sturdy support for grapevines to grow and produce fruit.

Guy Wires

NIZNAL® Vineyard Wire can be used to stabilise support 89posts, especially in windy regions. This ensures the structural integrity of the support systems, preventing damage from strong winds.

Pruning Wire

NIZNAL® Vineyard Wire can be used as pruning wire to secure pruned vines to the support system. Its durability and corrosion resistance make it a reliable choice for this application.

Canopy Management

NIZNAL® Vineyard Wire can be used to create a canopy management system, helping to control vine growth and optimize fruit production.

Repair and Replacement

NIZNAL® Vineyard Wire can be used to repair or replace damaged sections of existing support systems, extending their lifespan and ensuring continued support for the vines.

Hail Net Support

NIZNAL® Vineyard Wire can be used to support hail nets, which are essential for protecting grapevines from damage caused by hailstorms. It can be used to set up the hail net to ensure that the net remains taut and effective and provide optimal protection for the vines. This application is particularly useful in regions prone to hailstorms, as it significantly reduces crop losses.

NIZNAL® Vineyard Support Systems

Vineyard support systems are essential structures that provide a framework for grapevines to grow and bear fruit. They are crucial for maximising sunlight exposure, improving air circulation, and facilitating efficient vineyard management. These typically consist of posts, wires, and stakes that support the vines, preventing them from sprawling on the ground and becoming susceptible to diseases and pests. Grape farmers use these support systems to optimise grape quality and yield.

Y Support system

The NIZNAL® Y Vineyard Support system is a durable Y-shaped configuration that uses high-quality, corrosion-resistant wires to provide optimal support for grapevines. This system enhances vineyard organization, promotes fruit development, facilitates harvesting, increases yield, and improves air circulation for healthier vines and better fruit quality.

Mandap System

The NIZNAL® Mandap Support System is a durable, grid-like vineyard wire structure designed for high-density vineyards. It supports mature vines and withstands harsh weather. Its customizable design, using various wire gauges, accommodates different vine training systems and growing conditions. This system enhances grape quality and yield by providing consistent support and optimal airflow.
