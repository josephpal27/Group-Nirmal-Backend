ABOUT US

Group Nirmal, founded in 1971 by Shree Ram Autar Saraf, is a leading manufacturer of steel, aluminum, and zinc wires with an annual turnover exceeding ₹2000 crores and a production capacity of 216,000 metric tons. We serve various industries such as power, defense, and agriculture, while also trading metals to support India's infrastructure development. With over 50 years of experience and exports to more than 40 countries, we prioritize quality and innovation, adhering to national and international standards, positioning us as a leader in the Indian wire and cable industry.

OUR JOURNEY

1971

Beginning of wire drawing

Our journey began at our inaugural manufacturing facility in Belur under the Central Stores Supplies department.

1995

Welding electrode production

Beginning of a new production initiative to manufacture top notch welding electrodes under the name of Shieldarc Equipments.

1996

Ambika Metalliks

Embarking on a new venture to manufacture Hot rolled steel products.

2003

Launch of Tricon

A revolutionary high strength deformed bar that fortified our commitment towards quality and innovation.

2010

Commencement of Rudramani Project

Our first venture in real estate, the Rudramani Project, is now a major part of where Group Nirmal stands today.

2013

Deulti Plant Inaugration

New State-of-the-art Manufacturing facility of NWPL was established at Deulti which boosted production capacity to 4500 tons per month.

2014

MIG Wire Production

Kickstarting MIG Wire production & establishment of manufacturing unit 2 under Shieldarc Equipments.

2018

Completion of Rudramani Project

Marking the completion of Rudramani Project, Group Nirmal shifted corporate operations to Rudramani Builduing.

2019

First export container order from Deulti

Commencing our export operations, we shipped our first order from the Deulti plant.

2021

Commissioning of Second GI Line

This major development doubled production capacity at Deulti to 1,20,000 TPA

2022

Launch of NIZNAL®

Launch of NIZNAL® wires, a special zinc-aluminum coated steel wire.

2023

Launch of Nirmal Cables

Roll out of our first batch of cables which have now become a significant part of our Nation’s infrastructure.

2024

Acquisition of CeeDee Metalloys

Acquisition of CeeDee Metalloys in Bangalore. With an annual production capacity of 6000 MT, this manufacturing facility added Zinc Wires, Zinc-Aluminum Wires and Zinc Wire rods to our product portfolio.

2024

Acquisition of Khordha Plant

Acquisition of Hindustan Urban Infrastructure’s Khordha unit. This plant with an annual production capacity of 30,000 MT added Aluminum Wire rods to our portfolio & expands our capacity of Aluminum Conductor production.
